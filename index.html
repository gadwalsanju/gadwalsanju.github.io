About Me

Educator. Technologist. Mentor.

I'm Sanjana Gadwali, a Computer Faculty member at TGHSSS Jaitu (Govt. High School Jaitu), where I guide students through the fundamentals and advanced concepts of computer science, empowering them with skills for the modern digital world.

My professional journey began in the corporate sector with Reliance Communications, Jaitu, where I gained hands-on experience in telecommunications and technology operations. That real-world foundation deeply informs how I teach today — bringing industry relevance into every classroom lesson.

I believe that technology education, especially in government schools, is one of the most impactful ways to bridge the digital divide and open doors for young people across Punjab.
